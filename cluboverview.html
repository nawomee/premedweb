Club Overview

Pre-Med 2022-23


Mission Statement:

The Cardozo Premed club is dedicated to educate members on the different careers in medical field. Members will have the opportunity to engage in a research project of their choice in the duration of the school year. We will occasionally be joined by guest speakers or alumni to share their journey on the premed track.
